After all the rigorous academic pressure I always looked forward to this dance club. Where I could let the music flow through my blood. At first, I was very nervous and felt awkward when I danced in front of people. The dance leaders taught me different techniques to forget about the eyes and be confident when dancing. I have learned to be confident in my body. Another thing I noticed is that I lost some weight after starting to dance. When I do exercise my body tends to give up but when dancing it keeps moving because of the adrenaline. We performed in culture shock but in 2019 there was no culture shock, so we displayed our dance on Instagram live. The picture displays dance flowing with the the beautiful colors as I move
